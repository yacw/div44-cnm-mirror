Resources

CNM Task Force Petition to Support Relationship Diversity- A petition where you can also sign up for the APA Division 44 Consensual Non-monogamy Task Force mailing list.

CNM Task Force Petition to Support Relationship Diversity (Printable)- A printable version of the official Task Force petition to be shared in community and professional spaces (e.g., conferences, meetups).

CNM Task Force Flyer - A one-page flyer about the Div 44 CNM Task Force.

CNM for Mental Health Professionals Brochure - This printable handout created in collaboration with the National Coalition of Sexual Freedom is designed to help educate mental health professionals about consensual non-monogamy.

Sample Language for Assessing Relationship Style on Demographic Forms- One of the first steps to providing culturally competent care is amending client-history and intake forms to assure that they are inclusive toward marginalized populations, including those engaged in consensual non-monogamy (CNM). The following language provided by the APA Division 44 Consensual Non-monogamy Task Force is considered one of many inclusive ways to assessing relationship structure.

Consensual Non-monogamy Inclusive Practices Tool- A benchmarking tool to highlight inclusive clinical practices and policies related to equity and inclusion for people engaged in CNM.

CNM Therapist Brochure- A resource that can be used to help educate mental health providers about CNM.

CNM Healthcare Brochure- A resource that can be used to help educate medical health providers about CNM.

APA Division 44 CNM Task Force Initiatives- This is a list of the current initiatives of the Task Force.

CNM Literature List - A a list of indexed and organized peer-reviewed CNM articles for researchers and educators.

Kenneth R. Haslam Collection on Polyamory - A resource with historic polyamory artifacts that can be reviewed by researchers, educators, and the public.

Letter to APA Psychologist Locator - Our letter to APA Psychologist Locator requesting additional searchable categories (‘Consensual Non-monogamies/Open Relationships’ & ‘Kink/BDSM/Fetish Sexualities’) to their directory.

Letter to Psychology Today - Our letter to Psychology Today requesting direct searchable categories (‘Consensual Non-monogamies/Open Relationships’ & ‘Kink/BDSM/Fetish Sexualities’) to their directory.
